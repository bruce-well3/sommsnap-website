Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

SommSnap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Phone Number

We require your phone number for authentication purposes. This allows you to securely access your account and sync your wine preferences across devices.

Wine Preferences and History

We store your wine scanning history, preferences, and favorites to provide personalized recommendations and track your wine journey.

Camera Photos

When you photograph wine lists, the images are processed locally on your device. We only transmit the extracted text to our servers for analysis. Photos are not stored or transmitted.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized wine recommendations
  • To authenticate your account and enable cross-device sync
  • To improve our AI recommendation engine
  • To provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, a Google Cloud service. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and data storage
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered wine analysis and recommendations
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your wine history
  • Opt-out of promotional communications

Children's Privacy

SommSnap is not intended for users under 21 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
